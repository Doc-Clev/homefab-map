Site-Work Strip by Tier

Factory portion = 100% − strip%. Strip = excavation, foundation, utilities, permits, site prep, finish work outside the home shell.

Tier A: heavy regulation, seismic/coastal, high labor cost.
Tier B: northeast/west coast secondary.
Tier C: sun belt, mountain west, mid-Atlantic.
Tier D: south central, deep south, Appalachia.

Entitlement Assumptions
