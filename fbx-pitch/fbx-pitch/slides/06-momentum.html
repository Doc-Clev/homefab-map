The Strategy

Momentum first. Moats follow.

Defensibility doesn't exist on Day 0 — it emerges after a startup has already grown. Move fast, gain users, build leverage. The moat shows up once the conditions for it actually exist.

Stage 01

Momentum

Speed, growth, attention. Adoption loops. Fast iteration. The narrative.

→
Stage 02

Adoption

Users committing. First-mile contracts signed. Repeat behavior emerging.

→
Stage 03

Scale

Volume hits the threshold where unit costs drop and leverage compounds.

→
Stage 04

The Moat.

Defensibility emerges from accumulated scale. Now we're hard to dislodge.

Momentum
Short-term speed, growth, attention. Gets you noticed and funded.
Defensibility — the Moat
Long-term protection from competitors. Keeps you alive once everyone else notices.
