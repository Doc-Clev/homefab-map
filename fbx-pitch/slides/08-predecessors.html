Where Every Predecessor Failed

They tried to own the factory. We own the kit.

The Vertical Integration Trap

Every previous attempt tried to own design, factory, and construction. That requires hundreds of millions and rarely works. FBX is a software layer on the existing SIP manufacturer network. We scale by partnership, not by pouring concrete.

Mistake 01 · Structural

They tried to own the whole vertical.

Design, factory, and construction under one roof. $100M+ in capex before a single home delivers. FBX skips the factory layer entirely — partnerships with existing SIP manufacturers do the work.

Mistake 02 · Customer Experience

They sold a catalog of plans.

Lindal Cedar, Deltec, every modular brand — all forced customers to pick from a fixed menu. That's the boxed-in feeling that kills the category. FBX inverts this with a moodboard-first sandbox.

Software-on-top, partnership-scaled, moodboard-first. None of the predecessors had any of the three.
