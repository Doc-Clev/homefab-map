What Defensibility Looks Like

The four moats we're building.

NFX Framework

NFX identifies four defensibilities that matter most. Each requires scale — and momentum is what unlocks that scale. FBX is engineered for all four.

01

Network Effects

Value grows as more participants join the network.

FBX Application

More franchisees means denser delivery coverage, more installer capacity, and richer market data — which makes every additional franchise more valuable than the last.

02

Economies of Scale

Costs drop and pricing power grows with volume.

FBX Application

Aggregated procurement of panels, pods, and SIPs across 137 fabs unlocks pricing no regional builder can match. Hub-and-spoke logistics compound the advantage.

03

Brand

Trust and familiarity built through repeated customer exposure.

FBX Application

A standardized customer experience across every delivery market — Tesla / Carmax for housing. Repeat exposure turns "factory home" into a category we own.

04

Embedding

Becoming hard to replace through depth of integration.

FBX Application

Franchisees run on FBX systems — design, permits, kit, logistics, training. Switching out is replacing the whole operating stack, not just a supplier.

Each moat compounds with scale · Scale comes from momentum 19
