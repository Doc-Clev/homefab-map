The Pattern Repeats

Every great moat started as momentum.

The companies investors point to as having "unbeatable moats" today all started with raw speed and distribution. The defensibility came later — built on top of scale they earned by moving first.

Speed Distribution Scale Moat
Amazon
1994 · E-Commerce
Momentum
Cheapest, fastest book selection on the web. Loss-leader pricing to capture share.
Moat that emerged
Economies of scale. Massive buying volume → cost advantage no competitor can match.
Airbnb
2008 · Marketplaces
Momentum
Aggressive host onboarding, design-led trust, viral growth in cities one by one.
Moat that emerged
Network effects. Hosts and guests reinforce each other — a two-sided moat that compounds.
Booking.com
1996 · Travel
Momentum
Billions in performance marketing. Repeat usage. Relentless conversion optimization.
Moat that emerged
Brand trust. The default verb for booking travel — built one transaction at a time.
FBX HomeFab
2026 · Factory-Built Housing
Momentum
First-mover franchise rollout. AI-assisted permits. Hub-and-spoke from Day 1.
Moat we'll earn
All four. Network effects across franchisees, scale economics on the kit, brand on the customer side, embedding through systems.
