Why Now

Trends indicate growth.

  1. Sprawl is expensive — capital is shifting to PHX, Utah, Texas.
  2. Urban infill is hot — ADUs and density development.
  3. Starter homes in high demand — sub-$300K per door.
  4. Factory demand from large national builders.
  5. National & California laws now permit factory-built homes.

Modular is not new.

And modular's track record is the definition of insanity.

Factory production is not new.

Cars, phones, food — everything is built in a factory. If the trend is smaller, faster, more efficient, the answer is a factory.

Operating Principle R&D and infrastructure spend grows linearly. Output grows exponentially.
