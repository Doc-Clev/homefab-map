How HomeFab Solves It

Three distinct focuses. One system.

Solving Direction · Demand → Kit → Install

The retail market signals demand. FBX Core ingests it, standardizes it into a kit, and ships it to the Home-Fab. The Last Mile installs. Each layer is a distinct discipline — but they only work as one operating system.

The Pattern

People want to trust the brand but want the uniqueness of iterative design. Automobiles cracked this fifty years ago — standardized platforms, configurable trims. HomeFab is the same idea, applied to housing.
